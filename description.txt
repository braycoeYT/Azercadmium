This mod strives for unique and fun content, and has new ores, a ball of dirt, a new gamemode + leveling system, 2 new subclasses, an easier Slime Crown recipe (and truffle worm recipe), flare gun buff, crimson alternates, tons of vanilla recipes, and some hardmode enemies dropping prehardmode loot.
Note: The wiki is really outdated.
